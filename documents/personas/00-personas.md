## Overview
This document defines the core user personas for Veyya's two-sided marketplace. These personas are grounded in research from the Thailand market study, competitive analysis, and our value proposition for premium, accessible home services.

## Booker Personas (Clients)

### 1. Amira — Expat Professional

**Demographics**
- Age: 32
- Location: Bangkok (Sukhumvit area)
- Occupation: Marketing Director at a multinational company
- Income: 150,000–200,000 THB/month
- Language: English primary, basic Thai
- Living situation: Rented condo, lives alone or with partner

**Jobs-to-be-Done**
- "When I have a busy week ahead, I want to book reliable at-home beauty services so I can save time commuting to salons and maintain my self-care routine."
- "When I'm preparing for work events or social occasions, I need predictable, high-quality service so I feel confident and don't worry about last-minute cancellations."
- "When I want to try a new service provider, I need transparent reviews and verified credentials so I can trust them in my home."

**Goals**
- Book convenient at-home beauty services (nails, hair, makeup) without disrupting work schedule
- Access predictable, salon-quality service at home
- Build a relationship with trusted, recurring service professionals
- Minimize time spent managing appointments and payments

**Frustrations**
- Last-minute cancellations from independent providers found via Instagram or word-of-mouth
- Hygiene concerns with unverified providers
- Unclear pricing and hidden fees
- Language barriers with local providers
- Inconsistent service quality requiring constant provider switching
- No centralized way to manage bookings, reviews, and payments

**Motivations**
- **Convenience**: Values time over money; willing to pay premium for reliability
- **Self-care triggers**: Payday treats, special events, maintaining professional appearance
- **Social proof**: Influenced by recommendations from expat community and social media
- **Quality of life**: Seeks experiences that reduce stress and add comfort

**Trust Signals**
- ✓ Verified professional credentials and certifications
- ✓ High star ratings (4.5+) with substantial review counts
- ✓ Clear photos of past work and service portfolio
- ✓ Response rate and acceptance rate transparency
- ✓ Secure, escrow-based payment with clear pricing
- ✓ Hygiene certifications and insurance coverage
- ✓ English-language communication capability

**Success Metrics**
- Effortless rebooking with saved favorites (<30 seconds to rebook)
- Consistent 5-star experiences with trusted professionals
- Zero surprises: accurate time estimates and fixed pricing
- High rebooking rate (>60% within 30 days)
- Reduced mental load around service coordination

**Behavioral Patterns**
- Books 2–4 services per month (nails, hair, occasional makeup)
- Prefers weekend morning or weekday evening slots
- Books 3–7 days in advance
- High willingness to leave reviews and tips for good service
- Responds to loyalty programs and referral incentives

**Related User Stories:** BS-001 (Discovery), BS-002 (Booking), BS-004 (Favorites), BS-007 (Cancel/Reschedule)
**Related FRD Requirements:** R-DISCOVERY-001, R-DISCOVERY-002, R-BOOKING-001, R-BOOKING-006

### 2. Rania — Busy Parent & Household Manager

**Demographics**
- Age: 45
- Location: Bangkok (Thonglor or family-friendly suburb)
- Occupation: Part-time consultant, primary household manager
- Income: Household 250,000+ THB/month
- Language: English and Arabic fluent, conversational Thai
- Living situation: Owned home, married with 2 children and pets

**Jobs-to-be-Done**
- "When I'm managing my family's schedule, I need one platform for multiple services (beauty, pet care, occasional cleaning) so I can consolidate vendor relationships and reduce cognitive load."
- "When service providers are unreliable or don't show up, I need backup options and guaranteed availability so my household runs smoothly."
- "When I discover a great provider, I want to save them and rebook easily so I don't waste time re-searching every time."

**Goals**
- Manage all household services through a single, trustworthy app
- Access multiple service categories: beauty, pet grooming, occasional deep cleaning
- Coordinate services around complex family schedules (school pickups, activities, partner's travel)
- Build long-term relationships with reliable providers who understand household needs
- Minimize time spent on logistics and maximize time with family

**Frustrations**
- Juggling multiple apps, phone numbers, and payment methods for different services
- Unreliable providers who cancel or arrive late, disrupting family routines
- No visibility into provider availability, requiring back-and-forth messaging
- Difficulty finding pet groomers who will come to the home
- Having to re-explain household preferences (kids, pets, allergies) to new providers each time
- Payment friction and unclear tipping etiquette

**Motivations**
- **Family harmony**: Smooth household operations reduce family stress
- **Time optimization**: Values services that free up time for family and personal pursuits
- **Relationship building**: Prefers recurring providers who become familiar with the household
- **Peace of mind**: Willing to pay for verified, insured, trustworthy professionals
- **Self-care**: Occasional beauty services as respite from caregiving responsibilities

**Trust Signals**
- ✓ Family-friendly provider badges (experience with kids/pets)
- ✓ Background checks and KYC verification
- ✓ Insurance coverage for in-home services
- ✓ Clear cancellation and rescheduling policies
- ✓ Ability to save notes/preferences per provider
- ✓ Emergency contact and real-time service tracking
- ✓ Responsive customer support (WhatsApp/LINE integration)

**Success Metrics**
- Consolidated service management: all household services in one app
- Saved favorites: 3–5 trusted providers across categories
- Fast checkout: <60 seconds to book recurring appointments
- Reliable fulfillment: >95% on-time arrival rate
- Household satisfaction: positive feedback from family members
- Reduced mental load: reminders and automated rebooking options

**Behavioral Patterns**
- Books 3–6 services per month across categories
- Schedules pet grooming monthly, beauty services bi-weekly, cleaning quarterly
- Prefers morning or early afternoon slots when kids are at school
- Books 1–2 weeks in advance for planning certainty
- High loyalty to providers who deliver consistently
- Active in expat and parent community groups (word-of-mouth influence)

**Related User Stories:** BS-003 (Confirmation & Tracking), BS-006 (Multi-Service Management)
**Related FRD Requirements:** R-BOOKING-003, R-BOOKING-005, R-NOTIF-001

### 3. Praew — Young Local Professional

**Demographics**
- Age: 28
- Location: Bangkok (Ari, Ratchada, or near BTS)
- Occupation: Senior Accountant at a Thai company
- Income: 60,000–80,000 THB/month
- Language: Thai primary, English for work
- Living situation: Rented studio apartment, single

**Jobs-to-be-Done**
- "When I want to treat myself after a tough work week, I need affordable but quality beauty services at home so I can relax without the expense and hassle of premium salons."
- "When I'm comparing service options, I need transparent pricing and real reviews so I can make informed choices within my budget."
- "When I book a service, I want to support Thai women professionals like myself who are building independent careers."

**Goals**
- Access convenient, affordable beauty services (gel nails, eyelash extensions, massage) at home
- Find Thai-speaking providers within her budget range
- Support women-led, professional service providers
- Try new services and treatments she can't access in traditional salons
- Share recommendations with friends and colleagues for social validation

**Frustrations**
- Premium salons are too expensive for regular visits
- Freelance providers found via Instagram have inconsistent quality and unclear pricing
- Difficulty verifying credentials and hygiene standards of independent providers
- Awkward payment negotiations and tipping uncertainty
- No recourse if service quality is poor
- Fear of inviting strangers into her home without verification

**Motivations**
- **Value-conscious**: Seeks best quality-to-price ratio; appreciates transparent pricing
- **Social connection**: Books services with friends or shares experiences on social media
- **Self-improvement**: Sees beauty services as personal investment and confidence booster
- **Community support**: Prefers supporting independent Thai women professionals
- **Lifestyle aspiration**: Wants premium experiences at accessible price points

**Trust Signals**
- ✓ Thai language support and culturally familiar service descriptions
- ✓ Competitive, transparent pricing with no hidden fees
- ✓ Payment protection (escrow) so she only pays after satisfied with service
- ✓ Detailed provider profiles with credentials and photos of work
- ✓ Verified reviews from other Thai customers
- ✓ Clear cancellation policy with fair terms
- ✓ Promotions, loyalty rewards, and referral bonuses

**Success Metrics**
- Cost savings: 20–30% less than traditional salons for similar quality
- Service satisfaction: consistent 4+ star experiences
- Social validation: positive reactions when sharing on social media
- Booking frequency: able to afford 2–3 services per month
- Community building: successfully refers 2–3 friends who also book
- Empowerment: feels good supporting women-led businesses

**Behavioral Patterns**
- Books 2–3 services per month (nails, lashes, occasional massage)
- Prefers evening slots (after 6pm) or weekend afternoons
- Books 2–5 days in advance, sometimes same-day for spontaneous treats
- Price-sensitive but willing to pay for proven quality
- Active on Thai social media (Facebook, Pantip, Instagram)
- Influenced by promotions, first-time user discounts, and loyalty programs

**Related User Stories:** BS-002 (Booking with Escrow), BS-005 (Reviews), CS-002 (Localized Notifications)
**Related FRD Requirements:** R-BOOKING-001, R-BOOKING-002, R-REVIEW-001, R-NOTIF-001

## Provider Personas

### 1. Nok — Freelance Beautician

**Demographics**
- Age: 28
- Location: Bangkok (Ramkhamhaeng area, commutes citywide)
- Occupation: Freelance nail technician and eyelash extension specialist
- Income: 25,000–40,000 THB/month (variable, based on bookings)
- Language: Thai primary, basic English
- Living situation: Rents apartment with roommate, supports family in province

**Jobs-to-be-Done**
- "When I'm trying to build my client base, I need consistent booking visibility and high platform placement so I can earn steady income without spending on advertising."
- "When I complete a service, I need fast, transparent payouts so I can manage my cash flow and support my family."
- "When clients book through my profile, I need verified customers and secure payments so I don't risk no-shows or unsafe situations."

**Goals**
- Build a steady stream of bookings (4–6 per day) without relying solely on personal networks
- Increase earnings to 50,000+ THB/month through platform visibility
- Gain professional credibility through verified reviews and platform verification
- Access training and upskilling opportunities to expand service offerings
- Achieve financial stability and independence
- Maintain flexible schedule to balance work and personal life

**Frustrations**
- High platform fees (30%+) on other marketplaces without corresponding demand
- Delayed payments (2–4 weeks) creating cash flow challenges
- No-shows and last-minute cancellations without compensation
- Lack of safety verification for clients entering her workspace or visiting homes
- Limited visibility despite quality work and positive reviews
- No access to professional development or product discounts
- Difficulty managing bookings across multiple platforms and personal contacts

**Motivations**
- **Financial independence**: Building sustainable income to support family and personal goals
- **Professional growth**: Transitioning from informal work to recognized professional career
- **Skill mastery**: Pride in craftsmanship and delivering quality service
- **Client relationships**: Building loyal customer base through consistent excellence
- **Safety and respect**: Working in secure, respectful environments
- **Community belonging**: Connection with other women professionals in similar situations

**Trust Signals (from Platform)**
- ✓ Fair, transparent commission structure (25–30%, clearly communicated)
- ✓ Weekly payouts with clear earning breakdowns
- ✓ Customer verification and review system protects providers
- ✓ Platform insurance or safety guarantees
- ✓ Access to training modules and certifications
- ✓ High ranking algorithm rewards reliability and quality (not just volume)
- ✓ Transparent dispute resolution process

**Success Metrics**
- High visibility: appears in top 10 search results for her service category
- Consistent bookings: 20+ bookings per week
- Positive reviews: maintains 4.5+ star rating with >50 reviews
- Wallet transparency: can view pending, ready, and released earnings in real-time
- Repeat clients: >40% of bookings from returning customers
- Fair compensation: takes home 70% of booking value after commission
- Professional recognition: achieves "Veyya Verified" or top-tier provider status

**Behavioral Patterns**
- Accepts bookings 6 days per week, keeps Sundays for family
- Prefers clustered bookings in specific areas to minimize travel time
- Responds to booking requests within 15 minutes (high response rate)
- Invests in professional kit and branded appearance
- Active in provider community groups, shares tips and product recommendations
- Regularly checks earnings dashboard and payout schedule

**Pain Points Platform Can Solve**
1. **Route optimization**: suggest clustered bookings to reduce travel time
2. **Calendar integration**: sync with Google Calendar to prevent double bookings
3. **Automated reminders**: reduce no-shows with customer notifications
4. **Starter kit financing**: access to equipment through platform partnerships
5. **Skill development**: free or subsidized training modules
6. **Financial tools**: earnings tracking, tax guidance, savings features

**Related User Stories:** PS-001 (Onboarding), PS-002 (Availability), PS-003 (Accept/Reject), PS-005 (Earnings), PS-006 (Profile), PS-008 (Disputes), CS-001 (Role Switching)
**Related FRD Requirements:** R-PROVIDER-001, R-PROVIDER-002, R-CALENDAR-001, R-ESCROW-003, R-ESCROW-004

### 2. Somchai — Mobile Pet Groomer

**Demographics**
- Age: 34
- Location: Bangkok (Lat Phrao area, services multiple districts)
- Occupation: Independent mobile pet groomer (dogs and cats)
- Income: 35,000–55,000 THB/month (variable, based on bookings)
- Language: Thai primary, conversational English
- Living situation: Lives with partner, owns small van for mobile grooming setup

**Jobs-to-be-Done**
- "When I plan my daily route, I need geographically clustered bookings so I can maximize appointments per day and minimize fuel costs and travel time."
- "When customers book my services, I need accurate address information and clear access instructions so I don't waste time finding locations or dealing with access issues."
- "When I deliver excellent service, I need clients to rebook regularly so I can build recurring revenue and stable income."

**Goals**
- Optimize daily routes to serve 4–6 pets per day within efficient geographic areas
- Build base of recurring monthly customers (40+ regular clients)
- Maintain high service quality and customer satisfaction to earn positive reviews
- Reduce no-shows and late cancellations that waste scheduled time
- Increase average booking value through add-on services (nail trimming, dental care)
- Achieve consistent 60,000+ THB monthly income
- Maintain work-life balance by controlling service hours and days

**Frustrations**
- No-shows and last-minute cancellations waste prime booking slots
- Incorrect or incomplete addresses lead to delays and lost time
- Difficulty accessing gated communities or buildings without clear instructions
- Customers underestimate service duration, creating schedule compression
- Payment disputes or delayed payments for completed work
- Limited visibility compared to established brick-and-mortar grooming shops
- Fuel and maintenance costs eat into earnings during inefficient routing
- Seasonal demand fluctuations create income instability

**Motivations**
- **Route efficiency**: Values platforms that cluster bookings geographically
- **Predictable income**: Wants recurring monthly appointments from loyal clients
- **Professional recognition**: Takes pride in specialized mobile service offering
- **Animal welfare**: Genuine care for pet well-being and reducing pet stress from shop visits
- **Business growth**: Sees platform as way to scale beyond word-of-mouth referrals
- **Independence**: Values flexibility of self-employment with platform support

**Trust Signals (from Platform)**
- ✓ Verified customer profiles reduce no-show risk
- ✓ Cancellation policy includes compensation for late cancellations
- ✓ GPS-verified addresses and building access notes
- ✓ Calendar sync prevents double bookings
- ✓ Escrow payment guarantees compensation upon service completion
- ✓ Punctuality score and response rate showcased in profile to attract quality clients
- ✓ Geolocation-based search prioritizes him for nearby customers

**Success Metrics**
- Route efficiency: average 3+ bookings per geographic area per day
- Recurring revenue: 50% of income from monthly repeat customers
- Punctuality score: 95%+ on-time arrival rate
- Booking utilization: 80%+ of available time slots filled
- Customer retention: 60%+ of first-time clients rebook within 60 days
- Rating consistency: maintains 4.7+ stars with >100 reviews
- Income stability: <20% variance in monthly earnings

**Behavioral Patterns**
- Works Tuesday–Saturday (Mon/Sun off for maintenance and rest)
- Prefers morning starts (8am first appointment) to maximize daylight hours
- Accepts bookings up to 2 weeks in advance for route planning
- Blocks lunch hour for travel between areas
- Actively requests customer reviews after each service
- Uses platform analytics to identify high-demand neighborhoods
- Invests in professional van setup and branded uniform

**Pain Points Platform Can Solve**
1. **Intelligent routing**: algorithm suggests optimal booking sequences by geography
2. **Address verification**: require accurate GPS coordinates and access instructions
3. **Recurring booking automation**: offer customers monthly subscription with priority scheduling
4. **No-show protection**: cancellation policy with provider compensation
5. **Service packages**: enable add-on services (teeth cleaning, flea treatment) at booking
6. **Weather alerts**: notification system for outdoor setup challenges
7. **Vehicle expense tracking**: integrated mileage and expense logs for tax purposes
8. **Pet profile system**: save pet breed, size, temperament, and special needs for better preparation

**Related User Stories:** PS-004 (Service Completion), PS-007 (Route Optimization)
**Related FRD Requirements:** R-ESCROW-001, R-DISCOVERY-002 (distance calculations)

## Cross-Cutting Insights

### Trust & Safety Requirements
All personas prioritize trust and safety, but mechanisms differ:
- **Bookers**: Need provider verification, reviews, insurance, secure payments
- **Providers**: Need customer verification, payment guarantees, platform support for disputes

### Success Factors for Platform Adoption
1. **Ease of use**: <3 min booking flow (bookers), <30 sec to accept/reject (providers)
2. **Transparency**: Clear pricing, visible earnings, honest reviews
3. **Reliability**: High fulfillment rate, on-time arrivals, consistent quality
4. **Value alignment**: Fair commission (providers), competitive pricing (bookers)
5. **Community**: Social proof, referral networks, professional development opportunities

### Localization Needs
- **Language**: Thai primary with English support for expat segments
- **Payment**: Support PromptPay, Thai bank transfers, international cards
- **Cultural sensitivity**: Respect for Thai service culture and social norms
- **Communication channels**: WhatsApp and LINE integration critical for Thai market
