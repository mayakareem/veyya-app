# Risks, Assumptions, Mitigations

## Risks
- Provider supply scarcity
- Payment/payout failures
- Inconsistent quality

## Mitigations
- Early supply incentives, training partners
- Retry/backoff, reconciliation, finance dashboards
- Strict KYC, weighted ratings, audit alerts

## Assumptions
- Gateway supports split/connected payouts
- Calendar APIs available in target markets
