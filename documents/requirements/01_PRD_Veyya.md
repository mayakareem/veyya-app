# Veyya Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Executive Summary
Veyya is a two‑sided marketplace for on‑demand home services with a slight luxury angle (premium but accessible). MVP focuses on Beauty and Pet Services in Thailand first, designed to scale globally (UAE, SEA).

## 2. Goals & KPIs
- Avg. time to complete booking < 3 minutes
- Dispute rate < 2%
- Provider NPS > 60
- Repeat bookings > 40% by month 6
- Monthly GMV > USD 50k-equivalent by month 6

## 3. Users & Segments
- Clients: upper‑middle to affluent expats & locals seeking trustworthy, at‑home services
- Providers: verified professionals (beauty & pet), freelancers or micro‑businesses
- Admins: operations, finance, marketing, support

## 4. Scope (MVP)
- Client app: discovery, booking, escrow payment, live map, reviews
- Provider mode: onboarding/KYC, calendar & availability, accept/reject, wallet
- Admin portal: approvals, bookings, payouts, analytics, disputes

## 5. Positioning & Experience Principles
- Trust & curation over mass scale
- Effortless UX, minimal steps, clear pricing
- Privacy & safety by design (KYC, reviews, escrow)

## 6. Core Features
- Discovery & filters, provider ranking (distance, rating, response rate)
- Smart scheduling with manual availability + calendar sync
- Escrow payment; weekly payouts with commission 25–30%
- Ratings & moderated reviews
- Notifications (push/email/SMS)
- Admin analytics: GMV, cancellations, ratings, cohort retention

## 7. Non‑Functional
- Localization: EN/TH/AR; multi‑currency (THB, AED, USD)
- Performance: API <300ms p95, app load <2s on 4G
- Security: HTTPS, JWT, encryption at rest, RBAC
- Uptime: 99.5%

## 8. Phases
- P1: Design, APIs, wireframes (0–2 mo)
- P2: MVP build (3–5 mo)
- P3: Pilot Bangkok (6 mo, 50 providers)
- P4: Add categories + loyalty/referrals (8–10 mo)
