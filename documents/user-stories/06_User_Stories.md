# User Stories (MVP)

## Clients
- As a client, I want to filter providers by rating, price, and distance so I can pick the best fit quickly.
- As a client, I want escrowed payment so I feel safe paying in advance.
- As a client, I want reminders so I don't miss my booking.
- As a client, I want to rebook a previous provider in 2 taps.

## Providers
- As a provider, I want to set availability and connect my calendar so I avoid conflicts.
- As a provider, I want weekly payouts with a clear breakdown of commission and fees.
- As a provider, I want to accept/reject requests with a time limit to maintain my score.

## Admins
- As an admin, I want to review KYC documents quickly and consistently.
- As an admin, I want to see GMV, cancellations, and NPS at a glance.
