# Components & Services

- Auth Service (OTP, JWT, RBAC)
- User/Provider Service (profiles, KYC)
- Catalog/Service Service
- Booking Service (slot validation, state machine)
- Calendar Service (OAuth, sync, conflict detection)
- Payment/Escrow Service (intents, webhooks, wallet)
- Payout Service (batches, settlements, exports)
- Review Service (ratings, moderation)
- Notification Service (push/email/SMS)
- Admin/RBAC Service (roles, audit)
- Analytics/BI (dashboards, retention, cohorts)

Tech: Node.js/NestJS, Postgres, Redis, S3, React Native/Flutter, AWS/GCP
