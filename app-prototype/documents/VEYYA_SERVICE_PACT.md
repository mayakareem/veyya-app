# VEYYA STANDARD OF SERVICE PACT

## Commitment to Excellence for All Service Providers

At Veyya, we believe in empowering skilled professionals to deliver trusted, high-quality services with dignity and confidence. By joining our platform, every service provider becomes an ambassador of our values — quality, reliability, and respect.

---

## 1. PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT

- Arrive on time, dressed appropriately, and ready to provide excellent service.
- Treat all customers, their homes, and belongings with respect and care.
- Refrain from smoking, using offensive language, or engaging in any inappropriate behavior.
- Keep all customer information private and confidential at all times.

---

## 2. SERVICE QUALITY

- Deliver services that meet or exceed Veyya's quality and performance standards as defined in your training module.
- Use only approved tools, products, and techniques suitable for your service category.
- Confirm with the customer that the job is completed to their satisfaction before closing the booking.
- Maintain cleanliness and order — leave every space as you found it or better.

---

## 3. RELIABILITY & ACCOUNTABILITY

- Accept only the bookings you can commit to fully.
- In case of an emergency or delay, notify Veyya immediately through the app.
- Do not subcontract or transfer your bookings to others without written approval.
- Report any accidents, damages, or disputes within 12 hours of the incident.

---

## 4. SAFETY & HYGIENE

- Follow Veyya's Health, Safety, and Hygiene guidelines at all times.
- Maintain personal cleanliness, especially in beauty, wellness, and home-care services.
- Sanitize all tools, products, and equipment before and after each service.
- Follow safety measures for yourself and customers, including appropriate protective gear when required.

---

## 5. CUSTOMER FEEDBACK & RATINGS

- Treat all feedback as an opportunity for growth and improvement.
- Maintain an average rating of 4.5 stars or above to remain a premium provider.
- Repeated low ratings or serious complaints may result in retraining, temporary suspension, or removal from the platform.

---

## 6. VEYYA'S COMMITMENT TO YOU

Veyya is dedicated to building a trusted and supportive environment for all our service partners. We commit to:

- **Verified Customers**: We work only with verified customers to ensure your safety, trust, and peace of mind during every service.
- **Transparent Payments**: Guaranteed and timely payments for all confirmed bookings.
- **Support & Growth**: Access to continuous training, development programs, and marketing exposure to help you grow your business.
- **Fair Platform Practices**: Clear, transparent performance metrics and no arbitrary penalties.
- **Protection**: Our Veyya Safety Protocol provides emergency support and incident handling when needed.
- **Recognition**: Top-rated providers are celebrated through the Veyya Excellence Program, which offers rewards, promotions, and priority bookings.

---
