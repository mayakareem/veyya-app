## Key Learnings

### For Hi-So Customers
1. **Speed matters** - Under 2 minutes is table stakes
2. **Favorites are critical** - Save preferred providers for instant rebooking
3. **Trust signals work** - Verification and ratings reduce anxiety
4. **Transparency wins** - No surprises = happy customers
5. **Proactive communication** - Status updates reduce mental load

### For Product Development
- Pre-populate everything possible (address, payment, preferences)
- One-tap rebooking is powerful feature
- Smart reminders drive repeat usage
- Provider tracking creates confidence
- Automatic payment is luxury feature
