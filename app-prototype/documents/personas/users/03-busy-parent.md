# User Persona: Busy Parent

## Persona Overview

**Name:** Khun Nida "Nid" Pattana
**Age:** 38
**Location:** Ramkhamhaeng / Bangkapi, Bangkok
**Occupation:** Marketing Manager at Thai FMCG Company + Mother of 2
**Income:** ฿70,000-90,000 per month (household ฿150,000+)
**Living Situation:** Lives in townhouse with husband, 2 children (ages 8 and 11)

---

## Quote

> "Between work, kids' activities, and running a household, I'm juggling too much. I need services I can trust so I can be present for my family."

---

## Background

Nida is a working mother balancing career ambitions with family responsibilities. She and her husband both work full-time, coordinating childcare, school activities, and household management. She values reliability, safety, and services that help her family thrive without sacrificing her sanity.

---

## Demographics

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Marketing from Thai university
- **Family:** Married, two school-age children
- **Housing:** 3-bedroom townhouse in established neighborhood
- **Transportation:** Family car for school runs and errands, occasional taxi
- **Languages:** Thai (native), English (intermediate)
- **Social Status:** Upper-middle-class, active in school parent community

---

## Goals & Motivations

### Primary Goals
1. **Maintain household smoothly** - Keep home clean, organized, and functional
2. **Quality time with children** - Reduce time on chores, increase family time
3. **Reliable childcare support** - Trusted help for after-school care
4. **Safety and trust** - Vetted providers who can work around children
5. **Manage mental load** - Reduce stress of coordinating everything

### Secondary Goals
- Support local service providers who treat her family well
- Find consistent, regular help instead of one-time services
- Get recommendations from other parents
- Maintain work-life balance without compromising either

---

## Pain Points & Frustrations

### Current Challenges
1. **Time scarcity** - Managing work, kids, household leaves no personal time
2. **Safety concerns** - Worried about bringing strangers into home with children
3. **Reliability issues** - Past experiences with no-shows or last-minute cancellations
4. **Coordination overhead** - Managing schedules of multiple service providers
5. **Quality inconsistency** - Some providers great, others unprofessional
6. **Communication gaps** - Needs clear updates about service timing

### What Frustrates Them Most
- "I had to cancel an important meeting because the cleaner didn't show up"
- "I don't have time to interview multiple providers—I just need someone reliable"
- "Last provider was careless around my kids' things"
- "Why can't I book recurring services easily?"

## Behavior Patterns

### Service Usage
- **Frequency:** Uses services 2-3 times per week
- **Categories:** Cleaning (weekly), laundry, pet care, occasional repair services
- **Booking time:** Evening after kids sleep, or lunch break at work
- **Preferred booking:** Mobile app, but appreciates recurring booking feature
- **Payment:** Credit card or bank transfer, prefers auto-billing for regular services

### Decision-Making
- **Research:** Thoroughly reads reviews, especially mentions of reliability and professionalism
- **Price sensitivity:** Medium - willing to pay more for reliability and trust
- **Loyalty:** Highly loyal to providers who are consistent and professional
- **Referrals:** Heavily relies on and shares recommendations in parent groups

### Digital Behavior
- **Tech comfortable:** Uses apps daily but not tech-obsessed
- **Devices:** iPhone (usually 2-3 year old model), iPad shared with kids
- **Platform preference:** Mobile app for convenience
- **Notifications:** Wants service updates, reminders, and important info
- **Privacy:** Very concerned about children's safety and family data

## Service Priorities

### Must-Haves
1. ✅ **Verified, trustworthy providers** (safety paramount)
2. ✅ **Reliability** (on-time arrival, no last-minute cancellations)
3. ✅ **Recurring service scheduling** (weekly cleaning, etc.)
4. ✅ **Clear communication** (arrival times, updates)
5. ✅ **Easy rescheduling** (life with kids is unpredictable)

### Nice-to-Haves
- Provider notes (special instructions about kids, pets)
- Favorite providers list
- Family plan or package deals
- Same provider for recurring services
- Emergency/same-day service availability

### Deal-Breakers
- ❌ Unverified or sketchy providers
- ❌ Unreliable or frequently late
- ❌ Poor communication
- ❌ Can't accommodate regular schedules
- ❌ Unprofessional around children

## User Journey Touchpoints

### Discovery
- "I need a reliable weekly cleaner I can trust in my home with my kids"
- Finds Veyya through parent LINE group or Facebook recommendation
- Looks for trust signals and family-friendly features

### Consideration
- Reads reviews thoroughly, focusing on reliability mentions
- Checks if recurring booking is available
- Compares prices but prioritizes trust over savings
- May ask questions in parent community

### First Booking
- Starts with one-time service to test
- Books when kids are at school for first visit
- Carefully reviews provider profile and background
- Provides detailed notes about home/family

### Ongoing Usage
- If satisfied, books same provider weekly/biweekly
- Gradually expands to other service categories
- Becomes vocal advocate in parent networks
- Refers friends and family frequently
