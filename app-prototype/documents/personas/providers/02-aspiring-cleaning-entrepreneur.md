# Provider Persona: Aspiring Cleaning Entrepreneur

**Name:** Khun Pim "Pim" Siriporn
**Age:** 29
**Location:** Lat Phrao, Bangkok
**Business:** Started cleaning service 1 year ago (2-person team)
**Monthly Income:** ฿30,000-45,000 (growing)
**Business Model:** Partner with friend, mobile residential cleaning

## Background

Pim worked in hotel housekeeping for 6 years before starting her own residential cleaning service with a friend. She's hardworking, detail-oriented, and dreams of building a 5-10 person cleaning company. She's tech-capable but not tech-savvy. She needs business support—marketing, customer acquisition, and professional development.
