1. Establish a **sustainable and profitable proof of concept (POC)** in Thailand by 2025.  \
2. Leverage the same tech platform and processes for entry into **Singapore, Vietnam, and Malaysia** by 2026.  \

## 4. Country-Level Expansion Blueprints\
\
### 4.1 Thailand (POC Market)\
- Urban hubs: **Bangkok, Pattaya, Chiang Mai**.  \
- Partner: Local legal lead (Kate).  \
- Focus: Validate economics and ESG impact.  \
- Goal: Breakeven within 6 months and expand to 3 cities.  \
\
**Localization:**\
- Dual-language interface (Thai + English).  \
- Payment integrations: **PromptPay**, **LINE Pay**, **Rabbit Card**.  \
- Influencer-led marketing; SME partnerships for supply recruitment.\

### 4.2 Singapore (Regional HQ + Premium Market)\
- Entry Mode: 100% owned subsidiary (Singapore HoldCo).  \
- Positioning: Premium and corporate-focused (hotel and spa partnerships).  \
- Key Differentiator: Compliance, trust, and high-quality service matching.  \
\
**Target Audience:**\
- Expats, professionals, and wellness-conscious locals.  \
\
**Revenue Levers:**\
- Subscription bundles, corporate wellness programs, B2B tie-ups.  \
\
**Localization:**\
- English-only interface, cashless payment by default.  \
- Partnerships with **condos, serviced apartments, and HR tech platforms**.\

### 4.3 Vietnam (Emerging Market)\
- Entry Mode: Local joint venture (49/51 split).  \

- Risk: Complex bureaucracy; mitigate through local legal partnerships.  \

**Localization:**\
- Vietnamese interface + wallet integration (MoMo, ZaloPay).  \
- Local influencer campaigns and partnerships with beauty schools.  \
\
**Focus:**  \
Women\'92s empowerment narrative + training tie-ups with NGOs and academies.\
