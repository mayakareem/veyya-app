## Legal & Entity Structure\
\
### Recommended Model\
- **Parent Entity:** Veyya Pte. Ltd. (Singapore)  \
  - Owns IP, brand, and technology.  \
  - 50/50 ownership between Sindhu and Alex.  \
  - Handles fundraising, global partnerships, and licensing.\
\
- **Local Entity:** Veyya (Thailand) Co. Ltd.  \
  - 51% owned by Thai partner (Kate).  \
  - 49% owned by Singapore HoldCo.  \
  - Operates local business, manages compliance, and signs client contracts.\
\
### Equity & Profit-Sharing Framework\
- **Kate\'92s Equity:** 20% economic share in Thai entity, capped at local operations.  \
- **Profit Distribution Example:**\
  - Platform Commission = 30%\
  - Thai Entity Retains = 15%\
  - HoldCo (Singapore) = 15%\
  - Local Partner/Team Incentives = 5% (from retained margin)\
