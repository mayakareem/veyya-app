\f0\fs24 \cf0 # Veyya Business Model\
\
## Overview\
Veyya is a women-led, two-sided marketplace connecting customers with certified home and beauty service professionals through a technology-first platform.  \
It is designed to professionalize informal labor, improve service reliability, and create sustainable income opportunities for independent providers.\
\
---\
\
## Vision\
To become the most trusted women-led lifestyle platform \'97 redefining the home and beauty service industry by transforming informal work into professional, tech-enabled opportunities that empower women to build sustainable, independent livelihoods.\
\
---\
\
## Mission\
To enable women to earn, learn, and grow through accessible technology, verified opportunities, and a safe, transparent platform for delivering trusted home and lifestyle services.\
\
---\
\
## Problem Statement\
\
1. **Fragmented Access to Services:**  \
   The home services industry\'97covering beauty, cleaning, maintenance, and wellness\'97remains fragmented and offline, making it difficult for customers to access reliable professionals.\
\
2. **Inconsistent Quality and Low Trust:**  \
   Consumers face unpredictable quality, non-standard pricing, and lack of accountability. The regional market is worth over **$180B** yet lacks a single trusted player.\
\
3. **Limited Digital Enablement for Providers:**  \
   Independent professionals and SMEs lack digital tools, training, and steady client access, limiting their earning potential and business growth.\
\
4. **Evolving Urban Lifestyle Needs:**  \
   Modern consumers demand convenience, safety, and professionalism\'97but simplicity has become a luxury in the fragmented service ecosystem.\

## Value Proposition\
\
### For Customers\
- Access to reliable, high-quality professionals.\
- Transparent pricing and verified safety.\
- Convenience and recurring service bundles.\
- Premium experience with trusted professionals.\
\
### For Service Professionals\
- Steady work pipeline and increased visibility.\
- Fair pay (70% share of revenue) and flexible schedule.\
- Access to training, starter kits, and financial literacy tools.\
- Social and economic empowerment through digital inclusion.\
