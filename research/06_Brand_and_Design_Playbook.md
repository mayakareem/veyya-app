\f0\fs24 \cf0 # Veyya Brand and Design Playbook\
\
## Overview\
Veyya\'92s brand identity is designed to blend trust, elegance, and empowerment.  \
It targets urban, professional consumers seeking premium home and beauty services while promoting inclusion and opportunity for women-led service professionals.  \
The tone should be clean, confident, and modern \'97 balancing lifestyle appeal with operational credibility.\

## Visual Identity\
\
### Color Palette\
- **Primary:** Pastel beige (#F6E8DA), Coffee brown (#4B342F)\
- **Accent:** Sage green (#B6C2A2), Soft blush (#F9DCDC)\
- **Secondary:** Warm gray (#D8D4CE), Off white (#FAF8F6)\
\
**Guidelines:**\
- Use beige and coffee brown as the dominant pairing.\
- Keep visuals airy and minimal \'97 avoid dense patterns or dark contrasts.\
- Highlight real service professionals, not stock imagery.\
\
### Typography\
- **Primary Font:** Inter or Poppins (clean, readable sans-serif).\
- **Secondary Font:** Playfair Display (for titles, adding elegance).\
\
**Hierarchy:**\
- Titles: Large, confident (Playfair)\
- Subtitles: Clean and professional (Inter)\
- Body text: Highly legible and minimal.\
\

## Logo Direction\
- Wordmark logo with subtle feminine curvature and soft tones.\
- Prefer lowercase \'93veyy\uc0\u257 \'94 or stylized \'93VEYYA\'94 for modern appeal.\
- Minimal iconography (e.g., subtle abstract petal or spark motif).\
- No gold or metallic gradients \'97 keep it flat pastel for sophistication.\
\
---\
\
## Photography & Imagery\
- Use real people, genuine environments (urban apartments, Thai homes, natural light).\
- Feature professionals actively providing services (e.g., hair, nails, cleaning).\
- Avoid overly staged luxury settings \'97 authenticity over perfection.\
- Maintain gender-positive representation with diverse women.\

## Customer Experience (CX) Principles\
\
### 1. Simplicity\
Every customer interaction \'97 from booking to rating \'97 must feel effortless.  \
The UI should feature minimal steps, large buttons, and clear service categories.\
\
### 2. Trust\
Verified professionals, transparent pricing, and post-service reviews must be central.  \
Introduce a \'93Veyya Verified\'94 badge to signify top-tier providers.\
\
### 3. Empowerment\
Highlight the contribution of service professionals through profiles, stories, and ratings.  \
Include \'93Women of Veyya\'94 social campaigns.\
\
### 4. Delight\
Small surprises \'97 loyalty rewards, birthday offers, referral bonuses \'97 create emotional connection.\

## Training and Quality Standards\
\
### Onboarding Flow\
1. Online registration (ID, experience, service type).\
2. Video introduction & verification.\
3. Digital orientation module (2 hours).\
4. Test booking with internal team.\
5. Activation on the platform.\
\
### Continuous Quality Control\
- Ratings below 4.2 trigger re-training or review.\
- Mystery audits for top-tier clients.\
- Community meetups every quarter.\
\

- Emphasize balance, convenience, and professionalism.\
\

- **#HerTimeHerTerms** \'96 Spotlighting freelancers building independence.\

## Customer and Brand Consistency\
- Always maintain tone alignment: empathetic, reliable, and confident.\
- Avoid over-promising; emphasize quality over quantity.\
- Prioritize clear communication in multilingual (Thai + English) contexts.\
\
---\

## Key Takeaways\
- Visual identity must communicate warmth, credibility, and aspiration.  \
- Real stories build more trust than polished luxury marketing.  \
- Brand consistency across app, website, and social media is crucial.  \
- The onboarding kit and training framework reinforce brand professionalism.  \
- Empowerment narrative forms the emotional and strategic core of Veyya.\
